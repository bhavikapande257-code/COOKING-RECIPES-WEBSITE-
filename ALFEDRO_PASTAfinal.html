Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

ALFEDRO PASTA

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (preferably parmesan)
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Mixed vegetables (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook until soft. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and add chopped garlic.
  4. Sauté until fragrant, then add fresh cream.
  5. Stir well and add grated cheese.
  6. Mix until the sauce becomes creamy and smooth.
  7. Add cooked pasta and mix well.
  8. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Cook for 2–3 minutes until everything is well combined.

Now you can have your delicious pasta bowl
