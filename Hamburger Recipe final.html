HAMBURGER

Hamburger

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 hamburger patties
  • 2 cheese slices
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat a pan or grill on medium heat.
  2. Season the hamburger patties with salt and black pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 4 to 5 minutes on each side until fully cooked.
  4. Place a cheese slice on each patty and let it melt.
  5. Cut the burger buns into halves.
  6. Spread butter on the buns and lightly toast them on the pan.
  7. Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard on the buns.
  8. Place lettuce leaves on the bottom half of the bun.
  9. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese.
  10. Add tomato slices and onion slices on top.
  11. Cover with the top bun.
  12. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious hamburger.
