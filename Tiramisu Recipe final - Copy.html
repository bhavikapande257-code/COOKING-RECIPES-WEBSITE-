TIRAMISU

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

TIRAMISU RECIPE

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 200ml whipping cream
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare strong coffee and let it cool.
  2. In a bowl, beat mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  4. Whip the cream separately until soft peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Dip each ladyfinger biscuit quickly into the coffee.
  7. Arrange a layer of soaked biscuits in a dish.
  8. Spread a layer of cream mixture over the biscuits.
  9. Repeat the layers (biscuits and cream).
  10. Dust cocoa powder evenly on top.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4–6 hours or overnight.
  12. Serve chilled and enjoy your delicious tiramisu.
