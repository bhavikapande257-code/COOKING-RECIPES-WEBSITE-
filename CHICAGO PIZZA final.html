Chicago Deep Dish Pizza

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Difficulty Level: Hard

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp cornmeal
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup cooked sausage or vegetables
  • ½ cup pepperoni (optional)
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil for sauce

Preparation Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, olive oil, and cornmeal. Knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 to 2 hours.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the minced garlic.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, and salt. Cook for 10 to 15 minutes until thick.
  6. Grease a deep baking pan with oil or butter.
  7. Press the dough into the pan and push it up the sides.
  8. Add mozzarella cheese first, then add sausage, vegetables, or pepperoni.
  9. Pour the tomato sauce on top.
  10. Bake at 220°C for 25 to 30 minutes until golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes.
  12. Now you can have your delicious Chicago deep dish pizza.
