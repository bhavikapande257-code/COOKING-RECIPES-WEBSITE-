LOTUS BISCOFF CAKE

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Lotus Biscoff Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 200g Lotus Biscoff biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 300g cream cheese
  • 200ml whipping cream
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 150g Lotus Biscoff spread
  • Extra Biscoff biscuits for decoration

Preparation Steps

  1. Crush the Lotus Biscoff biscuits into fine crumbs.
  2. Mix the crumbs with melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into a cake pan to form the base.
  4. Refrigerate the base for 20–30 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  6. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  7. Whip the cream separately until soft peaks form.
  8. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture.
  9. Add Lotus Biscoff spread and mix until smooth.
  10. Pour the mixture over the biscuit base and spread evenly.
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4–5 hours or overnight.
  12. Decorate with melted Biscoff spread and biscuits.
  13. Now you can enjoy your delicious Lotus Biscoff cheesecake.
